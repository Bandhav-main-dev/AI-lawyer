Causing grievous hurt on Grave and sudden provocation, not intending to hurt and other than the person who gave the provocation

Description of IPC Section 335
According to section 335 of Indian penal code, Whoever voluntarily causes grievous hurt on grave and sudden provocation, if he neither intends nor knows himself to be likely to cause grievous hurt to any person other than the person who gave the provocation shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to four years, or with fine which may extend to two thousand rupees, or with both.


IPC 335 in Simple Words
Section 335 of the Indian Penal Code states that if someone voluntarily causes severe injuries in response to serious and sudden provocation, without intending or knowing it might harm anyone other than the person who provoked them, they can be punished with imprisonment for up to four years, or a fine of up to two thousand rupees, or both.

Punishment: 4 Years or Fine or Both
