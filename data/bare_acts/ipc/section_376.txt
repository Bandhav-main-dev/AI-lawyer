IPC 376 in Simple Words
In the Indian Penal Code, if someone commits a crime under section 376 and causes the death of the woman or leaves her in a permanent vegetative state, they can be punished with rigorous imprisonment for a minimum of twenty years, life imprisonment, or the death penalty.
