Kidnapping or abducting with intent secretly and wrongfully to confine a person

Description of IPC Section 365
According to section 365 of Indian penal code, Whoever kidnaps or abducts any person with intent to cause that person to be secretly and wrongfully confined, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.


IPC 365 in Simple Words
In the Indian Penal Code, anyone who kidnaps or abducts a person with the intention of secretly and wrongfully confining them can be punished with imprisonment for up to seven years and fined.

Punishment: 7 Years + Fine
