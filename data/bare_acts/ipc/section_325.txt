Voluntarily causing grievous hurt

Description of IPC Section 325
According to section 325 of Indian penal code, Whoever, except in the case provided for by section 335, voluntarily causes grievous hurt, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.


IPC 325 in Simple Words
Section 325 of the Indian Penal Code states that if someone voluntarily causes severe injuries to another person, they can be punished with imprisonment for up to seven years, and may also be fined.

Punishment: 7 Years + Fine
