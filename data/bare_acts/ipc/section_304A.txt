Causing death by rash or negligent act

Description of IPC Section 304A
According to section 304A of Indian penal code, Whoever causes the death of any person by doing any rash or negligent act not amounting to culpable homicide shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.


IPC 304A in Simple Words
Section 304A of the Indian Penal Code deals with causing death by a rash or negligent act not amounting to culpable homicide, and the punishment is imprisonment up to two years or a fine, or both.
