Extortion

Description of IPC Section 384
According to section 384 of Indian penal code, Whoever commits extortion shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three years, or with fine, or with both.


IPC 384 in Simple Words
According to section 384 of the Indian Penal Code, whoever commits extortion can be punished with imprisonment up to three years, or fined, or both.

Punishment: 3 Years or Fine or Both
