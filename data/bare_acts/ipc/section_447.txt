Criminal trespass

Description of IPC Section 447
According to section 447 of Indian penal code, Whoever commits criminal trespass shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three months, or with fine which may extend to five hundred rupees, or with both.


IPC 447 in Simple Words
If someone commits criminal trespass, they can be punished with imprisonment for up to three months, or a fine of up to five hundred rupees, or both.

Punishment: 3 Months or Fine or Both
