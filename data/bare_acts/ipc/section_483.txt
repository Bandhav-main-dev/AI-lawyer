Counterfeiting a property mark used by another, with intent to cause damage or injury

Description of IPC Section 483
According to section 483 of Indian penal code, Whoever counterfeits any property mark used by any other person shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.


IPC 483 in Simple Words
If someone counterfeits a property mark used by another person, they can be punished with imprisonment for up to two years, a fine, or both.

Punishment: 2 Years or Fine or Both
