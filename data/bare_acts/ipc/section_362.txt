

Description of IPC Section 362
According to section 362 of Indian penal code, Whoever by force compels, or by any deceitful means induces any person to go from any place, is said to abduct that person.


IPC 362 in Simple Words
In the Indian Penal Code, if someone uses force or deceitful means to make a person leave a place, it is considered abduction.
