Concealing or keeping in confinement a kidnapped person

Description of IPC Section 368
According to section 368 of Indian penal code, Whoever, knowing that any person has been kidnapped or has been abducted, wrongfully conceals or confines such person, shall be punished in the same manner as if he had kidnapped or abducted such person with the same intention or knowledge, or for the same purpose as that with or for which he conceals or detains such person in confinement.


IPC 368 in Simple Words
In the Indian Penal Code, anyone who hides or keeps a kidnapped or abducted person against their will shall be punished as if they were the one who kidnapped or abducted the person with the same intent or knowledge.

Punishment: As for Kidnapping or Abduction
