Description of IPC Section 403
According to section 403 of Indian penal code, Whoever dishonestly misappropriates or converts to his own use any movable property, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.


IPC 403 in Simple Words
If someone dishonestly takes or uses someone else's movable property, they can be punished with imprisonment for up to two years, or fined, or both.
