Being one of five or more persons assembled for the purpose of committing Dacoity

Description of IPC Section 402
According to section 402 of Indian penal code, Whoever, at any time after the passing of this Act, shall be one of five or more persons assembled for the purpose of committing dacoity, shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.


IPC 402 in Simple Words
If someone is part of a group of five or more people assembled for the purpose of committing dacoity (robbery), they can be punished with rigorous imprisonment for up to seven years and fined.

Punishment: Rigorous Imprisonment for 7 Years + Fine
