Marrying again during the life-time of a husband or wife

Description of IPC Section 494
According to section 494 of Indian penal code, Whoever, having a husband or wife living, marries in any case in which such marriage is void by reason of its taking place during the life of such husband or wife, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.


IPC 494 in Simple Words
If someone marries again while their previous husband or wife is still alive, and such a marriage is considered void, they can be punished with imprisonment for up to seven years and may also be fined.

Punishment: 7 Years + Fine
