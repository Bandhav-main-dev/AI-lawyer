

Description of IPC Section 481
According to section 481 of Indian penal code, Whoever marks any movable property or goods or any case, package or other receptacle containing movable property or goods, or uses any case, package or other receptacle having any mark thereon, in a manner reasonably calculated to cause it to be believed that the property or goods so marked, or any property or goods contained in any such receptacle so marked, belong to a person to whom they do not belong, is said to use a false property mark.


IPC 481 in Simple Words
If someone puts a mark on movable property or its container in a way that deceives others into believing that the property or goods belong to someone else, they are said to use a false property mark.
