Attempt to commit robbery or Dacoity when armed with deadly weapons

Description of IPC Section 398
According to section 398 of Indian penal code, If, at the time of attempting to commit robbery or dacoity, the offender is armed with any deadly weapon, the imprisonment with which such offender shall be punished shall not be less than seven years.


IPC 398 in Simple Words
If a person attempts to commit robbery or dacoity and is armed with a deadly weapon, they will be punished with imprisonment of at least seven years.

Punishment: Rigorous Imprisonment for not less than 7 Years
