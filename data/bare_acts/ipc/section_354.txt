Assault or use of criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty

Description of IPC Section 354
According to section 354 of Indian penal code, Whoever assaults or uses criminal force to any woman, intending to outrage or knowing it to be likely that he will there by outrage her modesty, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which shall not be less than one year but which may extend to five years, and shall also be liable to fine.


IPC 354 in Simple Words
Section 354 of the Indian Penal Code states that anyone who assaults or uses criminal force against a woman with the intention of outraging her modesty, or knowing that such an act is likely to outrage her modesty, can be punished with imprisonment ranging from one to five years and may also be fined.

Punishment: 1 to 5 years + Fine
