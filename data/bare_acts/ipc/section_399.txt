Making preparation to commit Dacoity

Description of IPC Section 399
According to section 399 of Indian penal code, Whoever makes any preparation for committing dacoity, shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment for a term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.


IPC 399 in Simple Words
If someone prepares to commit dacoity, they can be punished with rigorous imprisonment for up to ten years and fined.

Punishment: Rigorous Imprisonment for 10 Years + Fine
